Why import cars from the USA?

We compared the US markets with European ones and realized that buying a car in America is several times more profitable, no matter how paradoxical it may sound. This is caused by thoughtful logistics, the level of development of services for assessing the condition of the car and the very process of buying a car.

Most US citizens lease a car for several years and all the time of operation the leasing company itself is engaged in permanent maintenance of the car, as a result, cars from the USA are one of the best choices for motorists.

Why is the price so low?

Broken cars from the USA are bought from auctions of insurance companies. At these auctions, the car loses half the price even due to minimal damage. If we take into account the monetary costs, namely redemption, delivery, customs and repairs, then the price of a car similar in condition in Bulgaria will be 35-50% higher, and new ones will cost a lot of money.
